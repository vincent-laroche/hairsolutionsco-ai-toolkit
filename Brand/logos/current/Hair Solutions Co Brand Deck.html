Hair Solutions Co. V · Palette

Palette

Two colors. One mood.

Ink
#1a1815 · oklch(0.20 0.005 60)

Near-black with a warm undertone. Every wordmark and monogram uses this on light surfaces.

Paper
#f4efe6 · oklch(0.95 0.012 80)

Warm cream — not white. The primary surface for documents, decks, packaging, the site.

Paper · Deep
#ebe5d7 · oklch(0.92 0.018 85)

A half-step darker for cards, panels, and surfaces that need quiet separation without a hard border.

Paper · on Ink
#f1ecdf · oklch(0.93 0.015 85)

Slight cream-tint for reversed type on Ink — avoids the cold "pure white on black" look.
