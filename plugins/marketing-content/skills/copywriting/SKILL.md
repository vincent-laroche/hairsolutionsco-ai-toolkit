# Copywriting

Write conversion-focused copy for every marketing touchpoint — from headlines and CTAs to full landing pages and brand messaging.

## Core Copywriting Principles

1. **Write to one person** — always address "you," never "customers"
2. **Lead with benefit, not feature** — outcomes > specifications
3. **Specificity sells** — "32% more hair growth in 4 weeks" > "visible results"
4. **One message per page** — don't split attention
5. **Match language to your reader** — use words they use, not industry jargon

## Landing Page Structure

### Above the Fold (First Impression)
```
[Headline]: One sentence — primary benefit + transformation
[Subheadline]: Expand on the promise, add specificity
[Hero Image/Video]: Show the result, not the process
[Primary CTA]: Action word + benefit ("Book My Transformation")
[Trust signal]: "500+ clients in [city]" / "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.9/5"
```

### Body Copy Sections
```
1. PROBLEM SECTION
   - Acknowledge the pain point in their language
   - Show you understand (empathy before expertise)

2. AGITATION
   - What's at stake if nothing changes?
   - Make the cost of inaction vivid

3. SOLUTION INTRODUCTION
   - Introduce your product/service as the bridge
   - Not "we do X" but "you get Y"

4. HOW IT WORKS
   - Simple 3-step process
   - Reduce perceived complexity

5. SOCIAL PROOF
   - Transformation testimonials (before/after)
   - Specific results, real names, photos if possible

6. OFFER DETAILS
   - What exactly is included?
   - Price framing (daily cost / per session)

7. OBJECTION HANDLING
   - "What if it doesn't work?" → Guarantee
   - "Is it for me?" → Who it's designed for
   - "What's the catch?" → Transparent answer

8. FINAL CTA
   - Restate the transformation
   - Urgency/scarcity if genuine
   - CTA button with benefit language
```

## Headline Formulas

### Transformation Headlines
```
[Timeframe] to [Desired Result] Without [Common Obstacle]
"60-Day Hair Growth Program — Without Expensive Procedures"

From [Before State] to [After State]
"From Thin & Damaged to Thick & Healthy Hair"
```

### Curiosity Headlines
```
The [Counterintuitive Thing] That [Positive Outcome]
"The Morning Habit That Doubles Hair Growth (It Takes 3 Minutes)"

What [Authority/Expert] Doesn't Tell You About [Topic]
"What Most Hair Salons Won't Tell You About Keratin Treatments"
```

### Direct Benefit Headlines
```
Get [Specific Result] in [Timeframe]
"Get Noticeably Fuller Hair in 30 Days"

[Number] Ways to [Achieve Goal]
"7 Ways to Stop Hair Loss Before It Gets Worse"
```

## CTA Copy

### Instead of "Submit" or "Buy Now"
```
"Start My Transformation"
"Book My Free Consultation"
"Get My [Specific Result]"
"Reserve My Spot"
"See How It Works"
"Claim My [Discount/Free Item]"
```

### Micro-copy under buttons (reduces friction)
```
"No credit card required"
"Cancel anytime"
"Free 15-min call — no commitment"
"Join [N] clients who already transformed their hair"
```

## Product Page Copy

```markdown
# [Product Name] — [Transformation Promise]

[Opening line: Speak to the problem your buyer is experiencing]

**What makes it different:**
Unlike [common alternative], [Product Name] [unique mechanism] which means you [specific benefit].

**What you get:**
- [Benefit 1 — phrased as outcome: "Wake up with smoother, more manageable hair"]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]

**Ingredients / Key Features:**
- [Ingredient]: [Plain-language benefit — not chemical description]
- [Feature]: [What it means for the buyer]

**How to use:**
[3-step simple application]

**Results timeline:**
Week 1: [What they notice]
Week 4: [Visible improvement]
Week 8: [Full transformation]

**Guarantee**: [Risk reversal statement]

[CTA: "Add to Cart" or "Book Treatment"]
```

## Brand Voice Framework

Define your brand voice across 4 dimensions:

| Dimension | Scale | Choose One |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Tone | Formal ←—→ Casual | |
| Energy | Calm ←—→ Energetic | |
| Expertise | Accessible ←—→ Expert | |
| Personality | Corporate ←—→ Personal | |

### Voice Do's and Don'ts
```
We say:               We never say:
"transform"           "process"
"results"             "solutions"
"you deserve"         "our clients"
"proven"              "cutting-edge"
"we guarantee"        "we strive to"
```

## Price Framing Techniques

- **Daily cost**: "$199 treatment = $6.60/day for 30 days of transformed hair"
- **Cost of inaction**: "Trying 5 different shampoos at $30 each = $150. Our treatment = $199. Once."
- **Anchoring**: Show higher-priced option first, then mid-tier (which you want to sell)
- **Value stacking**: List everything included before revealing price

## Testimonial Format

Ideal testimonial structure:
```
"[Skepticism/Before state] — [Specific experience with product/service] — [Specific measurable result]. [Emotional outcome]." 
— [First + Last Name], [City] ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Example:
"I'd tried everything for my thinning hair with zero results. After just 6 weeks of the restoration treatment at Hair Solutions, I've had three people ask what I'm doing differently. My hair is noticeably fuller and I finally feel like myself again."
— Sarah M., Toronto ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
```
