# Social Media Content Creation

Craft platform-optimized content that drives engagement, builds community, and converts. Covers all major platforms with proven formats, templates, and strategies.

## Platform Strategies

### Instagram
- **Feed posts**: Transformation stories, product showcases, team spotlights (high-quality visuals)
- **Reels**: 15–60s tutorials, trending audio, behind-the-scenes — algorithm-prioritized
- **Stories**: Daily touchpoints — polls, Q&As, countdowns, swipe-ups, flash offers
- **Carousels**: 5–10 slides for tutorials, tips, before/afters — highest save rate → reach boost
- **Optimal times**: Tue–Fri 11am–1pm and 7–9pm

### TikTok
- Hook within first 1–3 seconds (state the problem or payoff immediately)
- Native vertical video, 21–34s optimal, up to 60s for tutorials
- Trending sounds boost discoverability
- Duets/stitches for engagement
- **Niche content over broad appeal** — algorithm rewards deep niche engagement

### LinkedIn
- Professional storytelling with personal narrative arc
- 3–5 sentence paragraphs, no walls of text
- Start with a hook line that creates curiosity or controversy
- End with a question to drive comments
- Articles and newsletters for authority building
- **Optimal times**: Tue–Thu 8–10am and 12pm

### Twitter/X
- Threads for complex ideas (1 tweet = 1 point)
- Conversational, punchy, opinion-led
- Quote-tweet strategy to join trending conversations
- Short-form value bombs (tips, one-liners)

### Facebook
- Community groups for organic reach
- Long-form storytelling still works
- Facebook Live for events and Q&As
- Local targeting for service businesses

## Hook Formulas

### Problem-Agitate-Solution
```
"Struggling with [specific problem]?
[Agitate — make the problem vivid]
Here's the exact [solution] we use at [brand]..."
```

### Pattern Interrupt
```
"Unpopular opinion: [counterintuitive take]"
"Nobody talks about [overlooked thing]"
"Stop doing [common behavior] — here's why"
```

### Curiosity Gap
```
"The [X]-step process that [result] (most people skip step [N])"
"What I learned after [timeframe] of [doing thing]"
```

### Social Proof
```
"[N] clients later, here's what actually works for [result]"
"Before/after: [Client name] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]"
```

## Post Templates

### Beauty/Hair Product Showcase
```
✨ [Transformation headline]

[2-3 sentences about the problem this solves]

Why it works:
→ [Benefit 1]
→ [Benefit 2]  
→ [Benefit 3]

[Call to action + link in bio or booking prompt]

#[niche hashtag] #[product hashtag] #[location hashtag]
```

### Tutorial Post (Instagram/TikTok)
```
[Hook: "How to [result] in [timeframe]"]

Step 1: [Action]
Step 2: [Action]
Step 3: [Action]

Save this post! You'll thank yourself later. 🔖

[Question to drive comments]
```

### Client Transformation Story
```
Meet [first name/pseudonym] 👇

[Before state — be specific and relatable]

She came to us [context].

After [treatment/product/timeframe]:
✅ [Result 1]
✅ [Result 2]
✅ [Result 3]

[Closing CTA]
```

## Content Calendar Structure

| Day | Content Type | Platform Priority |
|-----|-------------|------------------|
| Mon | Educational/Tips | LinkedIn, Instagram |
| Tue | Product/Service showcase | Instagram, TikTok |
| Wed | Behind-the-scenes | Stories, TikTok |
| Thu | Client transformation/testimonial | Instagram, Facebook |
| Fri | Engagement post (question/poll) | All platforms |
| Sat | Lifestyle/inspirational | Instagram |
| Sun | Repurpose top performer | Stories |

### Monthly Planning
- Week 1: Awareness content (attract new audience)
- Week 2: Education content (build trust)
- Week 3: Conversion content (drive bookings/sales)
- Week 4: Community content (build loyalty)

## Content Batching Workflow

1. **Theme week in blocks** — 2 hours per week
2. Write all captions first, then match to visuals
3. Use Notion or Google Sheets for content calendar
4. Pre-schedule 2 weeks ahead using Later/Buffer/Meta Suite
5. Leave 20% unscheduled for reactive/trending content

## Viral Content Framework (6 Steps)

1. **Find** — identify top-performing content in your niche using native search + SparkAds
2. **Analyze** — hook, structure, format, emotional trigger
3. **Extract** — what principle made it work?
4. **Adapt** — apply the principle to your brand/niche
5. **Enhance** — add your POV, data, or personal story
6. **Post + Remix** — publish, then create a follow-up (duet, reply, thread)

## Hashtag Strategy

- 3–5 niche hashtags (10k–500k posts) — high discoverability
- 1–2 broad hashtags (brand awareness)
- 1–2 location hashtags (local service businesses)
- Avoid banned hashtags — check via Instagram search

## Engagement Strategy

- Reply to every comment within 60 minutes of posting
- Pin the best comment to boost algorithm signals
- DM new followers with a genuine (non-spammy) welcome
- Engage with 5–10 accounts in your niche before posting
