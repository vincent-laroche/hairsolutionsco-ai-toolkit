# Content Research & Writing

Research-backed long-form content creation for blogs, articles, product descriptions, landing pages, and email newsletters.

## Content Research Process

### Step 1: Topic Intelligence
Before writing, gather:
- Primary keyword + 5–10 secondary/LSI keywords
- Top 3–5 ranking articles for the target keyword (analyze structure, not copy)
- Common questions (People Also Ask, Reddit, Quora, Answer The Public)
- Brand's unique angle or proprietary data point

### Step 2: Content Brief Template
```markdown
# Content Brief: [Article Title]

**Primary Keyword**: [keyword]
**Secondary Keywords**: [list]
**Target Audience**: [persona]
**Search Intent**: [Informational / Commercial / Transactional / Navigational]
**Content Goal**: [Drive bookings / Build authority / SEO traffic]
**Recommended Word Count**: [1,200–2,500 for guides; 500–800 for product pages]
**Key Points to Cover**: [list 5–8 points]
**Internal Links**: [2–4 pages to link to]
**CTA**: [What action should reader take?]
```

## Blog Post Structure

### SEO-Optimized Article Framework
```markdown
H1: [Primary Keyword + Hook — under 60 chars]

Introduction (100–150 words):
- Hook with relatable problem or surprising stat
- Preview what the article covers
- Implicit promise of transformation/value

H2: [Topic Section 1] (200–300 words)
H3: [Subtopic]
[Content + internal link opportunity]

H2: [Topic Section 2]
H3: [Subtopic with secondary keyword]
[Content]

H2: [Practical Tips / How-To Section]
[Numbered list or step-by-step]

H2: Common Mistakes / What to Avoid
[This earns featured snippets]

H2: FAQ (targets People Also Ask)
Q: [Question]
A: [2–3 sentence answer — concise for snippet]

Conclusion (100–150 words):
- Summarize key points
- Restate transformation/outcome
- Strong CTA
```

## Writing Principles

### Readability Rules
- Sentences: Average 15–20 words (max 30)
- Paragraphs: 2–4 sentences (never more than 5)
- One idea per paragraph
- Flesch-Kincaid score target: 60–70 (Grade 7–8 reading level)
- Use subheadings every 200–300 words

### Engagement Techniques
- Start with "You" — address the reader directly
- Use power words: transform, discover, proven, secret, instant, guaranteed
- Bullet points for lists of 3+ items
- Bold key phrases (not entire sentences)
- Include a quote or stat every 400–500 words

## Product Description Template

### Beauty/Hair Product Format
```markdown
**[Product Name]** — [One-line value proposition]

[Opening: The problem this solves, 1–2 sentences]

**What it does:**
- [Benefit 1 — outcome-focused, not feature-focused]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]

**Key ingredients/features:**
- [Ingredient/Feature]: [Why it matters in plain language]

**How to use:**
[Step 1–3 of application]

**Best for:** [Specific hair/skin type]

[Social proof element: star rating, "bestseller", customer quote]

[CTA: "Add to cart" or "Book treatment"]
```

## Email Newsletter Framework

### The 4-Part Newsletter
```
1. SUBJECT LINE (A/B test 2–3 variants):
   - Curiosity: "The hair mistake you're probably making"
   - Benefit: "3 ways to double your hair growth this month"  
   - Urgency: "Last chance: 20% off ends Sunday"

2. PREVIEW TEXT: Extends the hook (40–90 chars)

3. BODY:
   - Opening hook (1–2 sentences)
   - Core value content (tips, story, update)
   - Transition to offer
   - CTA button (1 primary CTA only)

4. PS LINE: Best read section — put secondary CTA or key insight here
```

## Content Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Content Type | Goal |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Awareness | How-to guides, trend articles, educational posts | Attract new audience |
| Consideration | Comparison articles, case studies, FAQs | Build trust |
| Decision | Product descriptions, testimonials, landing pages | Convert |
| Retention | Newsletters, loyalty content, tutorials | Keep customers |

## Pillar Content Strategy

Build topical authority with a hub-and-spoke model:
- **Pillar page**: Comprehensive guide on broad topic (3,000–5,000 words)
- **Cluster posts**: Specific subtopics linking back to pillar (1,000–1,500 words each)
- **Internal linking**: All cluster posts link to pillar; pillar links to clusters

Example for Hair Solutions Co.:
```
Pillar: "Complete Guide to Hair Restoration Treatments"
Clusters:
- "Keratin Treatment vs. Brazilian Blowout"
- "How Long Does Hair Restoration Take?"
- "Best Hair Treatments for Damaged Hair"
- "Hair Restoration Treatment Costs"
```

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing:
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, and meta description
- [ ] Meta description 150–160 characters with CTA
- [ ] All images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] At least 2 internal links + 1–2 authoritative external links
- [ ] Read aloud test — does it sound natural?
- [ ] Mobile preview — does formatting hold on small screen?
- [ ] CTA is clear and above the fold or in first scroll
