# Email Sequences & Automation

Design and write high-converting email automation flows for every stage of the customer journey.

## Welcome Series (5-Email Flow)

### Email 1 — Immediate (Send: 0 min after signup)
```
Subject: Welcome to [Brand] — here's your [lead magnet/discount]
Preview: Everything you need to get started

Body:
- Deliver the promised incentive immediately
- One-sentence brand story
- What to expect from future emails
- Reply prompt: "What's your biggest [problem related to your niche]?"
```

### Email 2 — Origin Story (Send: Day 2)
```
Subject: Why we started [Brand] (the honest version)
Preview: It's not what you'd expect

Body:
- Personal founder story focused on the problem you experienced
- Make the reader the hero (you were like them once)
- Introduce 1 key differentiator
- Soft CTA: explore most popular product/service
```

### Email 3 — Education (Send: Day 4)
```
Subject: The [X] mistakes most [target audience] make with [topic]
Preview: And how to avoid them

Body:
- 3–5 common mistakes (rooted in your expertise)
- Brief solution for each
- Build authority without being preachy
- No hard sell
```

### Email 4 — Social Proof (Send: Day 6)
```
Subject: "[Customer name]'s transformation" (use real story)
Preview: From [before state] to [after state]

Body:
- Client transformation story
- Before and after specifics
- How they achieved it (your product/service)
- Results in concrete terms
- CTA: See if this is right for you
```

### Email 5 — Conversion (Send: Day 8)
```
Subject: Your [discount/offer] expires in 48 hours
Preview: Don't leave this on the table

Body:
- Recap the transformation that's possible
- Urgency: offer expires
- Remove objections (FAQ format: "What if I'm not sure?")
- Testimonial or guarantee
- Strong CTA button
```

## Abandoned Cart Sequence

### Email 1 (Send: 1 hour after abandonment)
```
Subject: You left something behind 👀
Preview: Your [product name] is waiting

Body:
- Friendly reminder, not salesy
- Show product image
- "Was there a problem?" — lower friction
- CTA: Return to cart
```

### Email 2 (Send: 24 hours)
```
Subject: Still thinking it over? Here's what others say
Preview: Real reviews from real customers

Body:
- 2–3 relevant testimonials about the product
- Address top objection (price, results, fit)
- "We can answer any questions — just reply"
- CTA: Complete your order
```

### Email 3 (Send: 48 hours — optional discount)
```
Subject: Last chance — here's 10% off your cart
Preview: Offer expires at midnight

Body:
- Discount code
- Urgency (real deadline)
- Reinforce product benefit
- CTA: Use code [CODE] at checkout
```

## Post-Purchase Sequence

### Email 1 — Order Confirmation (Immediate)
- Order details + receipt
- Shipping timeline
- What to expect next
- "Thank you" that feels personal, not automated

### Email 2 — Onboarding (Day 3 after delivery)
```
Subject: Getting the most out of your [product]
Preview: Tips from our team

Body:
- How-to guide or usage tips
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Link to tutorials or knowledge base
- Invite to join community/follow on social
```

### Email 3 — Check-In (Day 7)
```
Subject: How are things going?
Preview: We'd love to hear from you

Body:
- Genuine check-in (not just fishing for reviews)
- Troubleshooting resource
- Invite for feedback (reply to email)
- Soft cross-sell of complementary product
```

### Email 4 — Review Request (Day 14)
```
Subject: Would you share your experience? 🌟
Preview: Only takes 60 seconds

Body:
- Ask for honest review
- Direct link to review platform
- Small incentive optional (loyalty points, discount on next order)
```

### Email 5 — Repurchase (Day 30–60, depending on product lifecycle)
```
Subject: Time to replenish your [product]?
Preview: You're probably running low

Body:
- Product reorder with 1-click
- New product suggestion
- Loyalty program invite if applicable
```

## Re-Engagement Campaign

### Win-Back Sequence (for inactive subscribers 90+ days)
```
Email 1: "We miss you" — genuine, personal tone
Email 2: "Has anything changed?" — update their preferences
Email 3: "Last chance to stay subscribed" — suppress if no open/click
```

## Subject Line Best Practices

### High Open Rate Formulas
- **Curiosity**: "The [thing] nobody tells you about [topic]"
- **Number**: "5 reasons your [thing] isn't working"
- **Personal**: "Quick question, [first name]"
- **Urgency**: "Closes at midnight"
- **Negative hook**: "What NOT to do with [product]"
- **Emoji**: Use sparingly at start or end — test performance

### A/B Test Variables
1. Subject line (always test this first)
2. Send time (try 10am vs 2pm vs 7pm)
3. Sender name ("Brand" vs "Founder Name at Brand")
4. Plain text vs HTML
5. CTA button color/text
