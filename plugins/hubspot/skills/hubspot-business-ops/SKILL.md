## How to Suggest Automations

When suggesting new workflows or automations, always consider:
1. Where in the customer journey does this apply?
2. What's the trigger event (purchase, inactivity, subscription change, etc.)?
3. What's the goal — retention, upsell, support deflection, re-engagement?
4. What CRM data enables this? (cross-reference hubspot-crm-model)
5. Effort estimate: simple workflow vs complex multi-branch sequence
