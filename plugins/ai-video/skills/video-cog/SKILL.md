# Video Content Strategy & Optimization

Strategic planning and optimization for video content across YouTube, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and website embeds.

## Video Content Framework

### The 3-Type Video System

**1. Authority Videos** (Long-form, 8–15 minutes)
- Deep educational content: "Complete Guide to Keratin Treatments"
- Behind-the-scenes: "A Day in Our Salon"
- Client journey documentaries
- Lives on YouTube; clips repurposed everywhere

**2. Discovery Videos** (Short-form, 30–60 seconds)
- Trending hooks, quick tips, transformation reveals
- Lives on Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Drives new audience acquisition

**3. Conversion Videos** (60–90 seconds)
- Product demos, testimonials, before/after reveals
- Lives on product pages, landing pages, email
- Drives direct action (booking, purchase)

## Video Script Framework

### Hook-Bridge-Body-CTA Structure

```
HOOK (0–5 sec): 
"[Surprising statement or bold claim]"
"[Question that creates immediate curiosity]"
"[Before/after reveal: 'This client had [problem]...']"

BRIDGE (5–15 sec):
"Here's what most people don't know about [topic]..."
"In the next [X] minutes, I'll show you [result]..."

BODY (varies):
[3–5 key points, one idea per beat]
[Visuals support every spoken point]
[Pacing: change visual or camera angle every 5–8 seconds]

CTA (last 15 sec):
"[Specific action] — link in bio / description"
"Comment [word] if you want [thing]"
"Save this for later"
```

## YouTube Optimization

### Video SEO Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in video title (natural, not forced)
- [ ] Title under 60 characters (no truncation in search)
- [ ] Keyword in first 2 sentences of description
- [ ] Full 5000-character description with timestamps
- [ ] Tags: 5–10 specific + 3–5 broad
- [ ] Custom thumbnail (see below)
- [ ] Chapters/timestamps in description
- [ ] End screen with subscribe + video suggestion
- [ ] Cards at video midpoint pointing to related content

### Thumbnail Design Formula

**High-CTR Thumbnail Elements:**
1. **Face with strong emotion** (surprise, excitement, curiosity)
2. **Bold text** — 3–5 words max, readable at thumbnail size
3. **High contrast** — subject stands out from background
4. **Color consistency** — match brand palette
5. **One clear focal point** — no clutter

**Text Formula Examples:**
- "[Result] in [timeframe]"
- "I [did thing] — here's what happened"
- "[NUMBER] [things]"
- "Stop [common mistake]"

### Chapters Format (Description)
```
0:00 Introduction
0:45 The main problem
2:15 Solution 1: [Topic]
4:30 Solution 2: [Topic]
6:45 Before and after results
8:00 How to get started
```

## Video Repurposing System

Turn one long-form video into 10+ pieces of content:

```
1 YouTube Video (10–15 min)
    ↓
├── 3 YouTube Shorts (60-sec highlights)
├── 5 Instagram Reels (15–30 sec clips)
├── 3 TikTok videos (key moments)
├── 1 blog post (video transcription → article)
├── 1 email newsletter (key insights)
├── 5 Twitter/X posts (one insight each)
└── 3 LinkedIn posts (professional angle)
```

## Platform-Specific Specs

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Length | Format |
|----------|-------------|--------|--------|
| YouTube | 16:9 | 8–15 min | MP4, H.264 |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | Under 60s | MP4 |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 15–90s | MP4, MOV |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 15–60s optimal | MP4, MOV |
| Facebook Feed | 1:1 or 16:9 | 1–3 min | MP4 |
| Website hero | 16:9 | 30–90s (loop) | MP4, WebM |

## Production Checklist

**Pre-Production**
- [ ] Script or detailed outline
- [ ] B-roll list (what visuals support each point)
- [ ] Thumbnail concept sketched
- [ ] Location and lighting setup planned

**Production**
- [ ] Stable shot (tripod or gimbal)
- [ ] Lighting: key light, fill light, background separation
- [ ] Audio: lavalier mic or condenser (not built-in camera mic)
- [ ] Multiple takes for key sections

**Post-Production**
- [ ] Edit for pacing (remove pauses, filler words)
- [ ] Captions/subtitles added
- [ ] B-roll inserted at every visual cut point
- [ ] Color grade consistent with brand
- [ ] Music at -30dB (background, not competing with voice)
- [ ] Intro under 10 seconds (get to value fast)
